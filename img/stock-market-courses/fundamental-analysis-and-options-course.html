Fundamental Analysis Concept
Chapter 1 - Why’s and What’s of Fundamental Analysis

Understanding the reasons for using fundamental analysis to assess a company's value. Learn the key elements and metrics for informed investment decisions.

Chapter 2 - ABC’s of Fundamental Analysis

You will receive an overview of all the terms and concepts to be covered in the upcoming chapters.

Chapter 3 - Key websites to know before the journey

Before going into the financial aspects, you will be introduced to key websites that will assist you on this journey.

Chapter 4 - Annual Reports

This crucial chapter outlines key elements to search for in a company's annual report which highly impact our analysis.

Chapter 5 - Introduction to Financial Statements

You will gain an understanding of various terms associated with financial statements, including market capitalization, face value, cash flows, and profit and loss (P&L) statements, among others.

Chapter 6 - Ratio Analysis

You will explore 5 key ratios commonly utilized in fundamental analysis. We will conduct an in-depth examination of their significance and examine their application in analytical processes.

Chapter 7 - How to identify the right company

You will learn about 4 tricks and 3 critical decisions essential for identifying the most suitable company.

Chapter 8 - Finding right Valuation

You will gain insight into various ratios that indicate whether a company is undervalued or overvalued.

Chapter 9 - Banking & Insurance Analysis

Banking and insurance companies operate under distinct parameters compared to other sectors such as FMCG and Metals. You will gain an understanding of the specific metrics and criteria used to analyze these financial institutions.

Chapter 10 - Portfolio Management

You will learn how to construct your own investment portfolio, including the key parameters to consider before creating one.
